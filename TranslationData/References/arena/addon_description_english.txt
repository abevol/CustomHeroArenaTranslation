Welcome to Custom Hero Arena!
Our official discord channel can be found at the link below: https://discord.gg/Fw7Qm2Z

Here you can choose and create a unique hero with finding the best abilities and combining them together! Mix and match Dota 2 abilities to create a war machine!

At the start of the game you will be given 1 random hero as well as 3 heroes to choose (one of each attribute).!
To win, you have to remain the last survivor within the 8-man lobby.
Duelling with other players one by one, betting on someone else's duel, fighting creeps that get stronger with every wave, buying unique items, finding the ultimate skill builds and much more can be found in Custom Hero Arena!

Play on! Get Better!
But most importantly, enjoy!
